The Best of Formula 1 2019

The 2019 season of F1 is shaping up to be a real classic. With the midpoint season underway the teams and drivers have lots to fight for.

THE BATTLE FOR SUPREMECY

Mercedes are in control

With 10 wins out of 13 for Mercedes, they are the team to beat. Winning 8 races in a row since the start of the season saw them become the first team in history to do so.

Lewis Hamilton has won 8 out of 13 grand prixs and has a healthy 60 point lead at the top of the standings with 8 rounds remaining. You could say he is well on his way to adding a 6th World Championship and only be one behind Michael Schumacher's tally of 7.

What has happened to Ferrari?

Pre-season favourites Ferrari would have hoped to be challenging for both championships but driver and team errors have seen them falter in recent races. Unfortunate mechanical issues for both drivers have seen them fade. In Bahrain, Charles Leclerc was confortably leading the race until an issue with the car meant he was beaten to victory by Lewis Hamilton and eventually finished 3rd behind the other Mercedes of Valtteri Bottas.
